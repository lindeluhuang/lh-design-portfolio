Your Health Research

Problem

In order for a new medicine or procedure to be adopted in a clinical setting, it needs to pass rigorous scrutiny through research studies that investigate the treatment’s effectiveness and safety. However, these clinical trials are often difficult to complete because volunteers need to spend their time and effort in order to participate.

There are many studies that never finish because not enough volunteers were found. As a result, many promising healthcare solutions never find their way to patients.

Proposal

The Your Health Research website is a platform where study teams can connect with people in the community who are interested in giving back to healthcare through participating in research studies. It began focused on the University of Michigan, where it now has over 50,000 research volunteers.

As the platform expands to other parts of the country like Chicago and Miami, we are currently assessing the needs and priorities for the next phase of the product.

Partners

We're working on this project in partnership with the Michigan Institute of Clinical & Health Research (MICHR) Participant Recruitment and Informatics teams.
