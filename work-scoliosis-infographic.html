Scoliosis Infographic Handout

Background

Scoliosis is unfortunately an extremely common condition that affects the curve of the spine. The treatment options are poor, and a group of interdisciplinary researchers at the University of Michigan wanted to come together to find a better way. This flyer was made for the brainstorming workshop, to help people focus on the key points of the scoliosis journey.

This page needed to feature key pain points of the scoliosis journey, while also containing necessary background to understand the disease. But because people would not have much time to look over it, there needed to be an easy way to find key information at a glance.
