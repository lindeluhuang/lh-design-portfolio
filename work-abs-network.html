ABS Network Website Redesign

Background

The Applied Biostatistical Sciences (ABS) network encompasses a group of applied statisticians across the University of Michigan. Often working as the only statistician within their team, this network allows for connections and resource sharing among the otherwise siloed staff.

The page was reaching a technical audience who came for a specific purpose and needed to quickly find what they were looking for. This made it so that the page content needed to fit above the fold on large screens, while featuring lots of links at the ready.

The original ABS website is shown below, followed by my redesigned version. The live site can be found at https://www.um-biostatnetwork.org/.
