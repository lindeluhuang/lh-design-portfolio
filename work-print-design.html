Print Design

Community Engagement Event Flyer Template - 3 Variations

The Community Enagement team often holds in-person events to bring academics and community members together. I created a series of flyer templates to help them professionalize and standardize their promotions.

Scoliosis Informational Poster

A group of interdisciplinary researchers gathered for a facilitated brainstorming event around improving the limited treatment options of scoliosis. Because some of the attendees did not have a medical background, I created an infographic to give a high-level overview of a person's journey with desease, highlighting the human elements that make the disease so agonizing.

MICHR Design Process Template

Many of my team's clients do not have any experience with design or the human-centered design process. I created this process overview template for my team to show to clients in order that they may better understand the work we do. It emphasizes the need for user feedback at almost every step, and can be customized for each project.

Team Advancement Award Flyer

Many research studies comprise of large, dispersed teams who do not typically have the chance to form a sense of unity. I created this flyer to promote the Team Adavancement Award, a sum of money given to teams who wish to invest in their team comraderie.

Good and Bad Design Analysis

My greater department focuses on research infrastructure, and few people have little knowledge of visual design principles. I created these charts as a way of highlighting a few key design principles to help my colleagues enter the wonderful world of design.
