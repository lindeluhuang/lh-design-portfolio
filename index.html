Hi there! I'm Linde.

I’m a Human-Centered Designer at the University of Michigan Hospital.

As part of the Design & Innovation team at MICHR, I help get important health discoveries from the research lab to the patient bedside.

My team provides strategy and design services all across the hospital to help teams gain a deeper understanding of their researcher and patient audiences and experiment with new approaches to better serve those audiences.

I strive to share the serendipitous creativity that comes from the principles, mindsets, and methods of human-centered design.
