Community Engagement Event Flyer Templates

Background

The Community Engagement team works to connect health researchers at the University of Michigan with community organizations so they can partner together on research projects. They frequently hold events for these two groups, and needed templates for publicizing their events.

Because of the variety and scope of the Community Engagement events, the flyer templates needed to be extremely flexible and versatile. I wanted to maintain the University of Michigan brand colors, but keep the page interesting and use photography for pops of interest and color. And of course, the key event information needed to be found easily and reliably.
